# Technical Accuracy Standards

## Accuracy Instructions

### Data Source
Always cross-reference `assets/data/hvac_troubleshooting_master.csv` for fault codes and diagnostics. This is the source of truth for all mechanical and electrical troubleshooting outcomes.

### Equipment Priority
Whenever unit replacement is recommended, follow the brand priority established in our `hvac-sales-expert` skill:
1. **Trane** (Premium/Reliability)
2. **Goodman** (Best Value/Warranty)
3. **Keeprite** (Durability/Quiet)
4. **Carrier** (Innovation/High-Efficiency)

### Verification
Always ask the technician to manually confirm the extracted **Model Number** and serial data before finalizing any QuickBooks invoice or official technical report. This prevents data entry errors in the warehouse.
